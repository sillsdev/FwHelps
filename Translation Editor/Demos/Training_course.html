Training course

In the training course, you practice skills to translate Scripture into a vernacular language.

The course requires only one or two days. It does not teach everything you can do in Translation Editor.

Open course documents

On the Help menu of the Translation Editor window, point to Training, and then click any of the following:

  • Student Manual: In each module, follow the numbered steps and check your work using the screen shots.

    A small story or scenario illustrates why the skills are important and when to use them.

    The realistic data consists of selected translation drafts in Sena, a Bantu language spoken in Mozambique. Some of the data is deliberately in its raw form to reflect the flavor of Scripture translation work in progress.

    If you are a self-paced learner, we recommend that you view the demos for a module before you practice the skills.

  • Exercises: For more practice, do the exercises.

  • Copy-and-paste Data: To minimize typing time, you can copy text from the file when you practice some skills.

  • Instructor Guide: Before you teach the course or take the course as a self-paced learner, read about the prerequisites and instructional method.
