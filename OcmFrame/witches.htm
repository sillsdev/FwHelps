754.2 Witches

Description

Recognition or detection of witches (i.e., how is a witch recognized by the people?); process of becoming a witch (e.g., inherited, special rites, etc.); abilities of witches; punishment for witchcraft (e.g., banishment, death, etc.); power of witches; etc.
