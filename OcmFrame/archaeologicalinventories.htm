915 Archaeological Inventories

Description

Listings or inventories or artifacts or non-artifactual constituents of a site, unit, trench, or stratum; statements about the number of artifacts or non-artifactual constituents of a particular type recovered (1100 flaked-stone tools were found at the site, 50 bird bones were found in unit A, etc.) etc.

Introduced in 1997.
