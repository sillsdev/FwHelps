792 Prophets and Ascetics

Description

Prophets and messiahs; ascetics and anchorites; monks, friars, and nuns; monastic orders (e.g., mendicant, hermit, cenobite); monasteries and convents (e.g., organization, activities); monastic rules (e.g., poverty, celibacy); characteristics, status, and means of support; etc.

The name of this category was changed from "HOLY MEN" to "PROPHETS AND ASCETICS" in 2000.
