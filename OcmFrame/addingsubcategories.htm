Adding Subcategories

Introduction

You may want to add subcategories to an OCM category to accomodate special needs you have in classifying things.

Example

In languages spoken by people for whom fishing is a major source of food, there may be many distinctions made in fishing-related activities, with a correspondingly complex lexicon. You might want to subdivide 226 Fishing into the following subcategories:

  • 226.1 Fishing methods
  • 226.1.1 Netting
  • 226.1.1.1 Net casting
  • 226.1.1.2 Net dragging
    •
  • 226.1.2 Spearing
