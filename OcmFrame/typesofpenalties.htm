696.1 Types of Penalties

Description

Social penalties (e.g., ridicule, gossip, avoidance, expulsion, dislike, hostility, ostracism, etc.); economic penalties (e.g., payments, loss of property control, deprivation of garden, etc.) religious penalties (e.g., withdrawal of protection, curses, etc.); physical penalties (e.g., beating, spanking, maiming, death, etc.).
