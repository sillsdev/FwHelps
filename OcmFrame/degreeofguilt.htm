695.1 Degree of Guilt

Description

Degree of guilt for infraction; factors in determining degree of guilt (e.g., motives, etc.); persons who are guilty (e.g., individual who committed infraction, persons who are guilty by association, etc.); persons who are held responsible for infraction (i.e., are slaves, women, children, animals held responsible?); persons who take on the responsibility of another's guilt; etc.
