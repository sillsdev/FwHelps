622 Community Heads

Description

Existence of community chiefs or heads; qualifications; mode of selection (e.g., by seniority, informal acceptance, election, matrilineal or patrilineal succession); functions and activities; domestic establishment; special status and functions of head's relatives; authority; prerogatives; etc.

The name of this category was changed from "HEADMEN" to "COMMUNITY HEADS" in 2000.
