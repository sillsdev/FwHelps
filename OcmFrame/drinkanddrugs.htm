270 Drink and Drugs

Description

General statements covering several aspects of the allaying of thirst and special appetite cravings, with the manufacture of artificial means of satisfying them, and with the preparation of drugs for other purposes.

The name of this category was changed from "DRINK, DRUGS, AND INDULGENCES" to "DRINK AND DRUGS" in 2000.
