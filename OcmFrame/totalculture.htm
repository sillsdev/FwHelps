180 Total Culture

Description

Summary statements on the total culture as analyzed, interpreted, or described. The 18* categories are intended to include descriptive and interpretive materials which pertain to the entire culture or which override a number of more specific categories. Consequently much of the material pertinent to the individual topics below will be found only under other headings.

Subcategories

  • 183 Norms
  • 184 Cultural Participation
  • 185 Cultural Goals
  • 186 Cultural Identity and Pride
