FRAME Categories

Description

FRAME Categories adapts the OCM for field research projects by grouping the categories of the OCM into eleven high-level categories. For more on FRAME, see Framework for Research and Methodology in Ethnography.

FRAME categories

  • PV Project Variables
  • GC General Culture and Change
  • MC Material Culture
  • EC Economics
  • SL Social Life and Identity
  • SG Social Groups
  • PP Political Process
  • AR Adversity and Resolution
  • WR Worldview and Religion
  • LC Life Cycle
  • TX Texts and Other Categories

Standard OCM categories

See OCM Categories for a list of the major categories in the Outline of Cultural Materials, 2002.
