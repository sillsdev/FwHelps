765 Mourning

Description

Duration of the mourning period; behavior of spouse and other relatives after the funeral (e.g., seclusion, mourning gab, observance of taboos, sacrifices); treatment of relics (e.g., preservation of skull or head, wearing of bones, use of air for artifacts); visits to the grave; mortuary feasts; exhumation, second funeral, and reburial; ceremonies terminating mourning; etc.
