5331y Melody

Description

Melody is a succession of musical tones which have pitch and duration (RHYTHM). Melody is the horizontal element of MUSICAL TEXTURE, whereas harmony is the vertical element. The pitches of a melody can be mapped according to the shape of the motion of a melody.
