5341a10 Air chamber

Description

Reed instruments can also have an air reservoir or air chamber.

Bagpipes have an air chamber in the form of a bag.

Concertinas, accordians, harmonicas, and pipe organs use air chambers. They are considered free-reed instruments because there is a reed for each pipe or note desired.
