621.4 Leadership Within Community

Description

Types of leadership (e.g., counsel, chief, different leadership for various activities, etc.); authority of leaders (e.g., authority over specific aspects of community life, limitations of authority, etc.); types of authority (e.g., inherited, delegated, achieved, etc.); function and activities of leaders; qualifications of leaders (e.g., based on economic wealth, power, kinship, religious status, etc.); relationship of spiritual leadership to community leadership, etc.
