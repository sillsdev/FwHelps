245 Arboriculture

Description

Specialized cultivation of trees and shrubs (e.g., woody perennials) for their fruits, nuts, berries, or other Data on the cultivation of trees for edible products (e.g., apple, breadfruit, coconut, cacao, coffee, orange, raspberry, tea, hops); special care and treatment of each (e.g., grafting, picking, drying, packing); viticulture; silviculture (e.g., raising of trees for wood pulp, soil anchor, screens, shade, decoration); organization of labor and production; etc.
