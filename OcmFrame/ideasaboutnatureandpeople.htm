820 Ideas about Nature and People

Description

General statements covering several types of speculative and popular notions concerning phenomena of the external world and of the human organism. Vernacular terms or names for any of these phenomena will be included in the appropriate "Ideas about Nature and People" (82*) categories rather than in "Vocabulary" (192).
