Some uses of the OCM in different components of a language project

Component Use
Language and culture learning
  • Select OCM categories as cultural domains to explore for vocabulary.
Literacy
  • Collect stories and texts based on OCM categories.
  • Encourage authors to write stories related to cultural topics in the OCM.
  • Collect data on 221 Annual Cycle activities that can be consulted when making yearly plans.
Community development
  • Collect information about OCM topics related to community leadership and decision-making as a basis for facilitating CD projects.
Translation
  • Develop information relevant to translation by focusing language learning and research on OCM topics found in Scripture.
