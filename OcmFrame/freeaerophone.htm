5341a12 Free aerophone

Description

In free aerophones the air is not confined by the instrument. The air is set in vibration by the whirling of a sharp-edged device, made of animal bone, stone, bamboo, metal, plastic, or wood.

Examples: 
  • Bullroarers
  • Spinning humming tops
  • Buzzing discs
