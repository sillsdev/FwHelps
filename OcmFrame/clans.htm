618 Clans

Description

Presence of localized kin groups consisting of unilinearly related persons of one gender and their spouses and young children but not including their out-marrying siblings of opposite gender; structural type (e.g., matri-clans, patri-clans, avuncu-clans, amita-clans); size (e.g., clan-barrios, clan-communities); number and distribution; subdivision (e.g., into subclans, extended families); organization; headship and succession; functions e.g., economic, political); reciprocal relations between members ; cohesive and fissive tendencies; land holdings; relations between clans; etc. Only data on clan-barrios will normally be indexed with this term.
