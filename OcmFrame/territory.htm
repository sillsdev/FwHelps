621.1 Territory

Description

Territorial boundaries for the society; limitations or conditions for outsiders to settle within territorial boundaries; importance of territory (e.g., what the society's territory means to them economically, historically, religiously, etc.), allocation of territory (e.g., a hunter's rights to the land he hunts on, given by leaders, inherited), etc.
