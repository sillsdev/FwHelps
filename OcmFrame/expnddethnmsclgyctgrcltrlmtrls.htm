by Kenneth R. Hollingsworth

Description

To help researchers make better distinctions in categorizing words and observations related to ethnomusicology, this Enhanced OCM has added subcategories and identifying numbers for the Music category in the Outline of Cultural Materials. With just a few exceptions, these subcategories follow those suggested in the prose description of the OCM.

Ethnomusicology subcategories

  • 5331 Music subcategories
  • 5341 Musical instruments subcategories
