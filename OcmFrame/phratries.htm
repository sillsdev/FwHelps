615 Phratries

Description

Presence of one or more unilinear kin groups, each of which embraces or more sibs; affiliation (e.g., matrilineal, patrilineal); regulation of marriage (e.g., exogamy, agamy, endogamy); distinguishing characteristics; origin and traditions organization; prerogatives and obligations of members; relations between phratries; etc.
