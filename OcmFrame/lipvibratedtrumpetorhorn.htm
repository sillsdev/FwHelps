5341a11 Lip vibrated (trumpet or horn)

Description

Lip-vibrated aeorophones are sounded by compressed buzzing lips, sometimes played into a cup mouthpiece.

Western instruments played with buzzing lips are called brass instruments, but in addition to metal, they can be made of wood, bamboo, animal horn, or other material.
