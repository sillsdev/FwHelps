304 Body Alterations

Description

Scarification and tattooing; cranial deformations; tooth filing and removal; piercing of ears, nose, and lips; genital mutilation (e.g., castration, circumcision, clitoridectomy); age, gender, and status differences; special techniques and apparatus; associated beliefs; etc.

The name of this category was changes from "Mutilation" to "Body Alterations" in 2000.
