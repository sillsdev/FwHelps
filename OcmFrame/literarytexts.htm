539 Literary Texts

Description

Texts, translations, and abstracts of folktales, myths, songs, and other individual literary products; critical analyses and commentaries thereupon; etc.

This term was used until 1997 when it was replaced with the categories listed under "Texts" (900). For information on texts indexed after 1997 use "Texts" (900) and its subcategories.
