776.2 Supernatural Beings

Description

Beliefs about supernatural beings such as high gods; superhuman forces (e.g., heaven, earth, sun, rainbow, etc.); natural forces (e.g., wind thunder, etc); unnamable spirits residing in trees, air, water, etc.; gods; heroes; ancestors; animals; totems; angels; humans who possess supernatural power; non-humans who possess supernatural power; characteristics, needs, attributes, abilities, actions, etc.
