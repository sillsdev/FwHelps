778 Sacred Objects and Places

Description

Conception of sanctity (e.g., possession by an indwelling or frequenting spirit, infusion with impersonal supernatural power); idols and fetishes; extraordinary objects (e.g., bezoar stones, albino animals, mandrake roots); supported with a faith akin to that in religion (e.g., ethical systems, political philosophies); etc.
