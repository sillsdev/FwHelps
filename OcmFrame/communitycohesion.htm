621.2 Community Cohesion

Description

Shared loyalties within the community (i.e., that which binds the people together as a unit, such as their name, their stories of origin, etc.); process by which one becomes a member of the community (e.g., birth, adoption, naturalization, purchase, etc.); attitudes toward the in-group (i.e., those who are native to the community); attitudes toward the out-group (i.e., those who are not native to the community); attitudes of the out-group toward the in-group, etc.
