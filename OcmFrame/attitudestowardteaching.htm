876.3 Attitudes Toward Teaching

Description

Methods used in teaching; methods valued in teaching; methods used to communicate important messages; status of teachers; preferred learning environment (e.g., quiet, noisy); goals in learning and teaching; communication of goals to students; consequences when goals are not attained; rewards when goals are attained; motivations for learning (e.g. fear, reward); etc.
