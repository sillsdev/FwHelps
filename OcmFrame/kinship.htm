600 Kinship

Description

General statements dealing with several specific aspects of the general characteristics and component terminology of kinship systems and with the specific patterns of behavior prevailing between kinsmen who do not belong to the same nuclear o polygamous family. For kin relationships within these types of family structure see "Family Relationships" (593).
