5341e Electrophones

Description

This category is not part of the Sachs–Hornbostel system, but has been added to classify instruments where the acoustical vibrations are produced by electric or electronic means.

Examples: 

Here are some examples of electrophones:

  • electric guitars
  • keyboards
  • synthesizers
