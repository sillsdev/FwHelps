876.2 Types of Teaching

Description

Informal teaching (e.g., imitative teaching, parental instruction, etc.); locations where informal teaching takes place; content taught in informal settings; person within family of orientation who is responsible for informal instruction; formal teaching; (e.g., classroom instruction, corrective teaching, etc); locations where formal teaching takes place; content taught in formal settings; person within the larger social unit who is responsible for formal instruction; etc.
