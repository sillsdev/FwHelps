755.1 Ritual and Medicine

Description

Sources of illness, accident, or death (e.g., natural, supernatural); specific causes of illness (e.g., wrongdoing on the part of the patient or his relatives, evil wishes and magic directed toward the patient by a third party, the action of a spirit, etc.); treatment of illness (e.g., magic combined with medicine, appeasement of third party to remove magic, propitiation of an evil spirit, etc.); preventatives (e.g., magical measures to ward off evil, charms, etc.); specialists who cure illness; etc.
