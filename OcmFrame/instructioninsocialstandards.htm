867.1 Instruction in Social Standards

Description

Communication of social expectations (e.g., technical teaching, formal teaching, informal teaching); methods used in teaching social norms (e.g., stories with morals, proverbs, myths, legends, etc.); degree of clarity in explaining social expectations and standards of conduct; etc.
