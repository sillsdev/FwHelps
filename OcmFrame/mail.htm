205 Mail

Description

This category is used to index information on traditional mail services for paper documents and packages (e.g., collection, delivery); transportation of mail (e.g., by pony express, rail, air); special equipment; parcel post; postal organization and personnel; finance (e.g., postal rates, subsidies). The category also contains information on electronic mail services (personal, listservs), service providers, and fees; etc.
