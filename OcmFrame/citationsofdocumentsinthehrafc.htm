111 Citations of Documents in the HRAF Collection

Description

Full bibliographical citations to all documents included, with a brief abstract of each. Documents are numbered. This category is relevant only to the microfiche and paper collections. The bibliographical citations in the eHRAF collections precede the documents to which they pertain and the citations can be searched and retrieved in. In eHRAF, the bibliographical citations are called "citation", not 111. All citations for each culture can be retrieved as a group. The method of retrieval depends on the medium you are using.

This category does not appear in the electronic version of HRAF as a searchable OCM category. Instead, in the electronic version a full citation precedes each document. This category was called "SOURCES PROCESSED" until 1997.
