356 Housekeeping

Description

Information on the upkeep of dwelling and outbuildings (e.g., painting, repairs); routine indoor chores (e.g., housecleaning, bedmaking); unspecified routine domestic tasks that occur outdoor adjacent to a dwelling in archaeological contexts; use and care of household implements and appliances; etc.

The description was updated in 2000.
