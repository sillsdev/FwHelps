181.2 Themes

Description

Objectives and ideas which permeate almost every aspect of life, as evidenced by self-image; resistance to change; restraints and warnings to children; reasons for arguments between members of the society; factors surrounding security; factors surrounding status or success; reasons for dissatisfaction; expressions of violent hate or rage; fear of specific punishments; logic and process of reasoning; concerns and worries; expressions of insult or ridicule; etc.
