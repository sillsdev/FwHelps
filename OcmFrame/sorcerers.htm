754.1 Sorcerers

Description

Recognition or detection of sorcerers (i.e., how is a sorcerer recognized by the people?); process of becoming a sorcerer; abilities of sorcerers (e.g., transform into an animal shape, injure by the power of thought, injure by the evil eye, etc.); punishment for sorcery (e.g., banishment, death, etc.); power of sorcerers; etc.
