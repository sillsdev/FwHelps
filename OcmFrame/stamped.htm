5341i5 Stamped

Description

With stamped idiophones, the sound is produced by the surface on which the stamping takes place.

Sometimes the stamping sound is enhanced by wearing special shoes such as tap shoes or clogs.

Examples: 

Covered pits, curved boards, hard floors
