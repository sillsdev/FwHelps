810 Sciences and Humanities

Description

General statements dealing with several aspects of the use of logic, scientific experiment, and other exact methods as means to the attainment of knowledge, and with the intellectual products of such methods. To the extent that knowledge is international or cross-cultural, only its degree of development in the particular society, not its detailed content, will be included.

The name of this category was changed from "EXACT KNOWLEDGE" to "SCIENCE AND HUMANITIES" in 2000.
